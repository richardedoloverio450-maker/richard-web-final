BIOGRAPHY

The YouTube Discovery

Justin Bieber was discovered on YouTube by Scooter Braun. His early covers quickly gained attention and launched his journey into global fame.

Rise to Fame (2010)

With the release of “My World 2.0,” Bieber became a worldwide sensation. Songs like “Baby” made him one of the biggest teen stars ever.

The Comeback Era

Justin matured as an artist with albums like “Purpose” and “Justice,” earning respect and redefining his sound.
